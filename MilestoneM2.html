Matera, located in the Basilicata region, is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world.

Its ancient cave dwellings, known as "Sassi," have been carved into the rocky hillsides for centuries.

Walking through Matera feels like stepping back in time.

The labyrinth of narrow streets, stone houses, and ancient churches carved into the rock make this city truly unique.
